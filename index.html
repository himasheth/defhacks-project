OUR RESPONSE TO COVID-19

- Our Purpose -

The COVID-19 Pandemic has shaken the world. People have been stuck in quarantine for months. The pandemic has brought great adversity in many forms, whether it be mental health struggles in isolation or stock market crashes threatening to cause economic recessions. The goal of our project, [smiley face], is to be able to support those suffering with these issues through this time.
